What is SQUADGOO?

SQUADGOO is a technology platform/marketplace that helps job providers (companies, recruiters, and individuals) connect with job seekers (TFN employees and ABN contractors). We focus on fair opportunities and platform integrity through verification and active monitoring.

Why people trust SQUADGOO

  • Verified users (Blue Tick): identity and key documents are reviewed before verification is approved
  • Platform integrity: removal of false ads, dormant accounts, and fake reviews
  • Privacy-first: contact details are not shared until an offer is accepted
  • Pay-per-match: no subscriptions — the sender pays only when an offer is accepted

How it works

  1. Sign up and choose your user type
  2. Complete your profile and verification steps
  3. Search, match, and send/accept offers

Search options

  • Manual Search: structured search for controlled hiring
  • Quick Search: premium urgent hiring with live tracking (mobile-only)
