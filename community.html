Community Guidelines

SQUADGOO Pty Ltd (ABN 43 670 318 715) — Melbourne, VIC

Support: admin@squadgoo.com.au

Last updated: 08 Jan 2026

1) Respect and safety

  • No harassment, hate, threats, bullying, or abusive language
  • No discrimination or intimidation
  • Keep communication professional

2) No scams or fraud

  • No scams, impersonation, or misleading information
  • No fake profiles, fake reviews, or false advertisements
  • No requests for sensitive personal data unless required for lawful service delivery

3) Honest listings and profiles

  • Provide accurate profile and job information
  • Do not post duplicate or misleading job titles
  • Do not post content that violates laws or third-party rights

4) Platform integrity

  • Do not attempt to bypass verification or security checks
  • Do not misuse reporting tools
  • Do not encourage users to break SQUADGOO rules

5) Enforcement

SQUADGOO may remove content, restrict features, suspend accounts, or permanently ban users for violations. Permanent bans may apply for harassment, scams, criminal activity, high cancellation rates, inactivity, or non-compliance.

6) Report an issue

If you see suspicious activity or content, report it in the app or email: admin@squadgoo.com.au
